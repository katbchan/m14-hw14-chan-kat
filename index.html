About Me

Hi, Kat here! I’m a student in the University of Florida’s Master of Arts in Mass Communication program with a concentration in Web Design & Online Communication. I come from a legal background, having earned my undergraduate degree in Legal Studies and currently working at a law firm. While I have no prior experience in computer science, this program marks my first deep dive into the world of coding. The technical side of web development resonates with my logical, analytical mindset—while the creative aspects of design push me to think differently and tap into my love for the arts, fashion, and visual storytelling. For me, web design is the intersection of structure and creativity… even if my codes sometimes look more like modern art than functioning HTML. But I’m excited to keep learning and growing in this field.
